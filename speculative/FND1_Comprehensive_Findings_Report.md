## 1. Executive Summary & The Core Problem
The primary directive of **Phase FND-1** within Spectral Causal Theory (SCT) was to determine if the Chamseddine-Connes Spectral Action (the foundation of the Noncommutative Standard Model) could be formulated natively and directly on a discrete Lorentzian Causal Set.

## 2. Phase I: The Combinatorial Illusion (Numerical Simulation)
Our initial attempt involved building a Python numerical simulation (`run_sj_spectral_action.py`) of the Spectral Action on a 2D Minkowski causal set using the Sorkin-Johnston (SJ) Pauli-Jordan matrix $A = \frac{1}{2}(C - C^T)$. 

**The False Success:**
The simulation returned perfectly smooth, non-divergent macroscopic scaling:
*   $\text{Tr}(A^4) \propto N^{4.01}$
*   $|\text{Tr}(A^2)| \propto N^{2.01}$

**The Analytical Teardown:**
While the numbers converged beautifully, physical analysis proved this scaling to be mathematically trivial and physically invalid.
1.  **Combinatorial Triviality:** We rigorously proved that $\text{Tr}(A^2)$ is exactly $-\frac{1}{2}$ the number of causal links in the graph, and $\text{Tr}(A^4)$ counts the number of 4-element causal chains. 
2.  **Extensivity Violation:** A physical action describing gravity (like the Einstein-Hilbert action $S \propto \int R \sqrt{g}$) must be *extensive*, meaning it scales linearly with the macroscopic volume $V \propto N$. The simulation yielded an action scaling as $V^2$ and $V^4$. This implies a non-local theory where the energy of the universe grows as the fourth power of its volume. 
3.  **Operator Mismatch:** The Chamseddine-Connes action requires a *differential* operator (the Dirac operator $D \sim \Box^{1/2}$), whose trace extracts local curvature from the UV (high-energy) spectrum. The SJ operator $A$ used in the simulation is an *integral* operator (a propagator), which extracts non-local volume correlations from the IR (low-energy) spectrum.

*   **Numerical Audit:** Our simulations (`verify_weyl_law.py`) proved that the low-energy modes of the causal set indeed obey the continuum Weyl's Law flawlessly. 

## 4. Phase III: The Fatal Breakdown (The Seeley-DeWitt Collapse)
Despite the mathematical elegance of Spectral Truncation, a strict physical verification revealed a fatal flaw regarding the generation of General Relativity.

2.  **The Paradox:** To cure the graph noise, we *must* delete the UV spectrum of the causal set. But by deleting the UV spectrum, we physically delete the local curvature information. 
3.  **The Loss of Poles:** The trace of a spectrally truncated (finite) matrix has no geometric poles as $t \to 0$. Without poles, there are no Seeley-DeWitt coefficients. There is no $a_2$ term. **Gravity vanishes entirely.**

## 5. Phase IV: The Dual Paradox (The Final No-Go Theorem)

*   **Candidate 1: The Sorkin-Johnston Operator ($D_{SJ}$)**

Plugging it into the Spectral Action $\text{Tr}(f(B))$ yields divergent, complex, non-Hermitian mathematical garbage. It **fundamentally violates the axioms of Noncommutative Geometry**.
