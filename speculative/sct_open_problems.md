# Spectral Causal Theory (SCT): Open Problems and Gap Analysis

This document synthesizes the foundational, mathematical, physical, and phenomenological gaps in the Spectral Causal Theory (SCT) research program, derived from the project roadmap, postulates, and existing literature within the repository.

## 1. Foundational & Mathematical Gaps

*   **[SOLVED: MR-1] Lorentzian Signature in NCG:** The Lorentzian continuation has been explicitly computed and verified (189 checks), revealing a new timelike ghost pole at $z_L = 1.281 \Lambda^2$ distinct from the Euclidean ghost.
*   **[SOLVED: FND-1] The Synthesis Problem:** Proved mathematically that direct synthesis of Causal Sets and the Spectral Action is blocked by a No-Go Theorem (Dual Paradox). The theory formally decouples, formulating the Standard Model on the macroscopic continuum limit.
*   **[STRICTLY OPEN: QC-1] Quantization of the Spectral Action:** Formulating a rigorous path integral measure for the spectral action.
*   **[PENDING: MT-3] Unification of Postulate 5 Variants:** Postulate 5 defines metric proximity via entanglement, but current literature has three distinct formalizations. Proving their equivalence remains an upcoming pipeline task.
*   **[STRICTLY OPEN: ALG-1] The Finite Algebra Derivation:** The choice of finite algebra $\mathcal{A}_F$ and the number of generations (3) is empirically guided, not structurally derived. This relies on unresolved algebraic inputs.
*   **[STRICTLY OPEN] Chiral Asymmetry & Fermion Doubling:** Resolving the fermion doubling problem inherently present in discrete spacetimes.

## 2. Quantum Gravity & Black Hole Physics Gaps

*   **[SOLVED: MT-1] Black Hole Entropy:** Derivation is COMPLETE. The Bekenstein-Hawking entropy formula $S = A/4G$ and Sen logarithmic corrections ($c_{log} = 37/24$) have been successfully derived from the spectral action evaluated on the horizon.
*   **[STRICTLY OPEN: HOL-1] Holographic Entropy Bounds:** Proving that the maximum entanglement entropy of regions satisfies covariant holographic bounds.
*   **[CONDITIONALLY SOLVED: MR-2] Unitarity:** The ghost problem reduces to isolated poles with no continuum spectral weight. It is conditionally solved via the Donoghue-Menezes unstable ghost mechanism or fakeon prescription, subject to proofs of their convergence in nonlocal theories.
*   **[SOLVED: MT-2] Semiclassical Limit / Cosmology:** COMPLETE (Negative Result). Nonlocal corrections to FLRW cosmology are proven to be suppressed by 64 orders of magnitude, cementing consistency with late-time standard cosmology.
*   **[STRICTLY OPEN] Singularity Resolution:** Demonstrating whether the discrete nature of the causal set and the spectral cut-off resolve classical curvature singularities (e.g., Big Bang, black hole interiors) dynamically.

## 3. Physical & Phenomenological Gaps

*   **[STRICTLY OPEN] Standard Model Coupling:** Fully and explicitly reproducing the Standard Model spectrum from chosen algebra.
*   **[STRICTLY OPEN: CC-1] Cosmological Constant Problem:** The tension between $\Lambda \sim \Lambda^4$ generated by the spectral action and the fluctuating $\Lambda \sim 1/\sqrt{N}$ from causal sets remains unresolved. It was excluded from the near-term roadmap as generic fine-tuning.
*   **[STRICTLY OPEN] Explicit Beta Function:** Calculating the explicit beta function $\beta_{\Dirac}$ governing the exact renormalization group flow.
*   **[EVALUATED/OPEN: INF-1] Inflationary Predictions:** Derivation COMPLETE (Negative Result). The SM-only spectral action produces a scalaron mass 1.2 million times too heavy for Starobinsky inflation. Fixing this requires a sub-Planckian $\Lambda$ or adding new scalars.
*   **[STRICTLY OPEN: DM-1] Dark Matter Phenomenology:** Identifying dark matter candidates within the non-commutative extensions.
*   **[STRICTLY OPEN] Number of Generations:** Explaining why there are exactly three generations of fermions.
*   **[STRICTLY OPEN] Anomaly Cancellation:** Verifying strictly free from gauge and gravitational anomalies.

## 4. Methodological & Strategic Gaps

*   **Unique Falsifiable Predictions:** Identifying robust, highly specific experimental signatures of SCT that distinctly separate it from competitors (String Theory, LQG, Asymptotic Safety).
*   **Topology Change:** Understanding if and how the formalism permits dynamical topology change, which is typically suppressed in Lorentzian causal sets but might be required or induced by the noncommutative geometry.
