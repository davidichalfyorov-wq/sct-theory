\section{Structural Obstruction: $E^2+B^2$ vs $E^2-B^2$}

\begin{fact}[Type N Property]
On a pp-wave spacetime (Petrov type~N) with Brinkmann profile $H = \varepsilon(x^2-y^2)$, the electric and magnetic Weyl parts with respect to the diamond's timelike observer $n = \partial_t$ are:
\begin{equation}

\end{equation}
giving $E^2 = B^2 = 2\varepsilon^2$, $C^2 = 8(E^2-B^2) = 0$, $E^2+B^2 = 4\varepsilon^2 \neq 0$.
\end{fact}

This is the core obstruction: $C^2 = 0$ on type~N, but CJ $\neq 0$ (measured at $t > 8$ significance). Therefore CJ cannot be proportional to any functional of $C^2$.

\begin{proposition}[Structural Obstruction]

\end{proposition}
